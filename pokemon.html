ARCEUS

Arceus is mythical Pokémon.
Creator of the Pokémon world.
Holds immense power. Revered
as a deity.

LUCARIO

Lucario: Fighting/Steel-type
Pokémon. Aura master. Agile
and powerful. Aura Sphere
attack. Wise and loyal.

GRENINJA

Greninja is water/Dark-type
Pokémon. Ninja-like speed
and precision. Can change type
with "Protean" ability.

LUNALA

Lunala is psychic/Ghost-type
Pokémon. Moon spirit. Powerful
psychic energy. Moongeist
Beam. Symbol of the night sky.
